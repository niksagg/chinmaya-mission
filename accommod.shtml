Welcome
Welcome to Holiday Inn Willowbrook-Hinsdale Hotel, a premier corporate conference center hotel and an exceptional venue for your wedding receptions, family reunions, and religious retreats.

Experience this truly unique destination, with something for everyone. Conveniently located in the Western Suburbs of Chicago, Holiday Inn Hotel Chicago-Willowbrook/Hinsdale is accessible to all major expressways, Chicago's O'Hare International Airport and Chicago's Midway Airport (complimentary shuttle to and from Midway Airport on availability & must call to schedule pick up).

Neighboring the corporate communities of: Burr Ridge, Hinsdale, Oakbrook, Lemont and Woodridge, our full service hotel and conference facility provides top-level service and amenities to satisfy our business and leisure guests.

All of the 220 guest room accommodations and suites have been newly refreshed and boast brand new pillowtop mattresses and have many conveniences and amenities. Willby's restaurant and lounge, outdoor pool, exercise room with a sauna and guest laundry facilites add to a very comfortable stay for our guests while attending a conference or visiting family in the greater Chicago area.
