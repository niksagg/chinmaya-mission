Waterford Banquet and Conference Center

933 South Riverside Drive
Elmhurst, , IL 60126
Phone: 630-279-0270
Fax: 630-279-1240

The Waterford is only 12 miles away from the O’Hare International Airport and 15 miles from Midway Airport. The Waterford is ideally situated off of Route 83, barely a mile from the Oak Brook corporate shopping centers, also known as a shoppers paradise, which boasts several upscale boutiques and chic restaurants making it a perfect getaway during multi-day conferences or events. The Waterford is a popular choice for events as it is easily accessible from I-290, I-294, I-55, I-355 and I-88

» From O'hare Airport

Take I-294 South towards I-290 West.
Stay on I-290 West and take exit Route 83 South Proceed approx. 2mi, at the 5th traffic light turn left onto Riverside Drive and follow the sign towards the hotel

» From Midway Airport

Take I-55 South to Route 83 North. Go approx., 8 miles.
Turn Right onto Riverside Drive and follow the signs to the hotel.

» From Downtown Chicago

Take I-290 West to Roosevelt Road (Route 38) West Exit, Go 3 miles and exit at Route 83 North.
Turn right onto Riverside Drive and follow the signs to the hotel

» From Indiana/Ohio

Take I-80/90/94 West. Follow I-94 to I-294 North to Roosevelt Rd. (Route 38) West, go 2 miles and Exit Route 83 North.
Turn right onto Riverside Drive and follow the signs to the hotel.

» From Wisconsin

Take I-94 East to I-294 South (Indiana), and follow the directions from O'hare Airport.
