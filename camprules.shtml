Code of Conduct

We ask that you kindly follow these rules so that everyone gets the most out of the camp experience. We appreciate everyone’s respect and cooperation.

 

 

1.       Please attend all classes/activities and arrive on time.  Transportation to the ashram leave promptly every morning.

 

2.       Show good manners and consideration in your behavior towards your gurus and fellow students. Greet everyone with "Hari Om".

 

3.       Sleep on time so that you can wake up on time and feel well rested for a productive day. Hotel room curfew is 12AM.

 

4.       Comfortable clothing may be worn as long as they are appropriate to the ashram atmosphere. (Shirts must be worn at all times. Tops and bottoms must meet at the waist. Shorts and skirts must be of appropriate length. No halter tops, spaghetti straps or low cut neckline. Clothing should not display profanity or offensive slogans/symbols.) Indian clothes are preferred.

 

5.       Please respect other guests at the hotel (i.e. don’t be too loud).

 

6.       Do not leave the premises of the hotel or Chinmaya Badri Ashram without notifying appropriate people on the steering committee.

 

7.       Keep the hotel rooms and the ashram clean and orderly.

 

8.       No chewing gum during the session.

 

9.       No cell phones in classrooms or shrine. If you carry one, it should be turned off for the duration of the session.
